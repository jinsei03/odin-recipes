Scrambled Egggs

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make simple scrambled eggs!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk or water
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Place them in a medium bowl, and whisk until the yolk and whites are thoroughly combined. Add the milk or water, and whisk again! The beaten eggs should be an even yellow color, with no translucent spots or streaks.
  2. Next, gently preheat the pan. Brush a small nonstick skillet with olive oil, or melt a little butter inside it. Warm the skillet over medium heat.
  3. Finally, cook. Pour in the egg mixture, and let it cook for a few seconds, undisturbed. Then, pull a rubber spatula across the bottom of the pan to form large, soft curds of scrambled eggs.
